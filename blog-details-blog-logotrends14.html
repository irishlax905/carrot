5 Logo Design Trends Of 2014

  • Apr. 19 2014
  • Posted By: Carrot Press
  • Logo Design


As we emerge farther into 2014, we’ve seen some spectacular design trends in the logo and branding world take place. What’s highlighted is the vast array of each trend - differing from the other in such a way that allows designers to fulfill their creative outlets while delivering the customer exactly what they’re looking for. It’s a great year for design, here’s some trends that we’ve collected as some of the most popular of the season and taking 2014 by storm:



5. Oppa Icon Style!



In 2013 we saw out-there designs but in 2014, we are experiencing very friendly icons being used. What makes an icon cute and friendly in our minds are turning those letters into icons. Icons are those friendly bits of artwork representing a cause and effect - so what better for a corporate brand logo or even a small boutique or bakery to adopt a friendly icon-style logo for their image. For these trends, we are seeing bold lines, fun and simple shapes being used, as well as minimal amounts of typography, if any is present.




4. Handmade for the Earthly





The year of 2014 is the year to get in touch with nature; to ground ourselves as designers and remember where the sources of our creativity comes from - our minds and our hands - and there is in fact, a sort of beauty to those tools. Still, amongst all the modern tools we have readable in today’s day and age, we still rely on our minds, bodies and hands to create wonderful arrays of colors, text and shapes for familiar representation. In this style, we notice organic lines, forms and typography being utilized to create earthly function and form.

3. 90’s Throwback At It’s Finest





Throwing us back a decade or two, we have cartoons and geography, married together in a way that brings back childhood memories for most of us. Reminiscent as it may be, these trends we are seeing also bring a new and noteworthy wave of brilliance to the design world this year. With a fresh connection to our earthly trend previously, we are kindly reminded of this precious rock that we call our home and accompanied with bright, friendly colors to remind ourselves that we are not owners of this planet, and must treat it as an ally of ours as well. All of these aspects of bright colors, country, state and global shapes as well as cartoon style imagery brings this trend to our #4 spot for 2014.



2. Stack Those Letters, Baby





You guessed it, the stack. Perfect for brand names with a long word or two, and ideal for capturing the consumer’s attention. Exactly why would it capture the attention of onlookers? It’s the simple fact that the human brain strives to correct words that we see as ‘misspelled’, or at least, that is how we perceive the text at first. Our brains clamor together an explanation as to why the words are scrambled as such, almost taking on a puzzle without our conscious consents. As we solve the puzzle, it allows us to identify the name of the brand or company, and gives the brand enough time to put an image in the consumer’s head about who they are. Contrasting colors, and bold fonts are successful tactics for this popular trend of this particular Spring season of 2014.



And for our #1 most trendy style for logo design of 2014 - drumroll please!



1. Separated Serifs!





Separated serifs, yes the modified font that is taking the typography and logo design world by storm. A trend making it so modern due to the minor tweak of the typeface. Also, san serif fonts are nice to mix with this separated serif above as subtitle or category of product. A modern touch on the regular san serif fonts seen day after day. This trend is used perfectly for salons, beauty products, clothing stores, magazines and photography companies. Bring back the east coast fashion trend of the early 2000’s with this retro-modern trendy typeface.
