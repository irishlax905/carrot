10 Successful Ways to Rebrand and GROW Your Business!

  • Oct. 10th 2016
  • Posted By: Carrot Press
  • Our Company

As the business world grows, entrepreneurs are constantly working tirelessly to stay ahead of their competition. Overwhelmingly enough, there are several factors to consider when looking to rebrand your business; ultimately to get more leads, sales, exposure or whatever goal you are set out to reach. Or perhaps, it’s the circumstances in which you want to rebrand your business - you just moved, you are refurbishing or maybe you and your colleagues have simply come to the conclusion that your logo isn’t ‘fresh’ enough for new customers.



Whatever the reason behind undergoing a rebranding process may be, a rebranding process takes three main and very important things to remember, and of course, the easiest way to remember is to recall them as the ‘Three C’s’ and they go like this: cause, creativity, consistency.



CAUSE: Every Business has a Cause/Effect That Customers Thirst For

Let that marinate in your noggin for a while: If you can’t think of what your brand, company, conglomerate, enterprise, service or product does in terms of a cause and effect, then you should honestly consider another line of work. Yes, those are heavy words I understand however, your entity will literally be ‘eaten alive’ by your competitors, as Kevin O’Leary says, ‘You’ll be smashed like the cockroach you are’, if you think you can create a business without some sort of cause and in return, an effect or affect for this matter, on your customers. Really brainstorming the cause/effect of your company will then give you an image for your brand.



Once you’ve solidified that phase of 101 rebranding, and you are absolutely sure that you’ve come to a clear idea of what you are servicing, offering or advertising to your customers, it is now time to think of your name, icon or logo.



CREATIVITY: Redesign Your Vision



Icons, logos and slogans are truly the first and foremost that a consumer will see, take in and already form an opinion of your company - averaging roughly 30 seconds to 1 minute for a formulated opinion about you, your brand and what you’re representing. That is why your logo has to be strong, well-designed and smart to stay ahead of what is already out there. Consider these factors into your already existing color scheme or logo:

  • - Does it have mascot colors?
  • - What is the meaning of your slogan?
  • - Is your logo vectorized?
  • - Have you reached out to peers for opinions?


Selecting a great designer is truly a key factor to the success of your logo or scheme, as it is truly in the ‘eye of the beholder’ of great design. No one expects any entrepreneur business man or woman to pick up a pencil and paper and demand them to draw or design a logo in the time they are needing one, but rather; to be a smart investor in the wide variety of designers to choose from for an astounding design, and DO NOT settle until you get more than what you are looking for. Once you have that design? Put it on everything, mugs, t-shirts, pens, shirts for your employees - everything. That way, every consumer knows who you are, where you are going, and never questions the professionalism of your brand.



CONSISTENCY: Optimize That Website for SEO



It is just as vital to create a strong presence in the online world as it is in the physical world (i.e business cards, letter heads, etc.). You guessed it, your website, socials, etc. We as a society of entrepreneurs, understand it’s monotonous and almost seems asinine to put endless time into a website thinking, ‘it’s just a website, how can it matter so much?’. Every entrepreneur has asked themselves that question one time or another but, it is more important than we think.



Did You Know? Almost 40,000 people search for services online, PER HOUR on any given day


That’s a lot of customers for all our entrepreneurs out there! But the golden question is, how do you tap into that lush amount of customers that you’re drooling from the teeth to service and communicate with? The answer is NOT waiting around for them to come to your website but instead, EFFECTIVE search engine optimization (SEO) services.



Now, there are two methods to go about this: One is extremely risky and can be a dangerous game to play, and the other is much safer, but may take much more time to reach your SEO goals. The first step though, is to figure out how much you want to progress in the DA, (Domain Authority: your rank in Google Algorithm). Once you’ve found out if you want to rank via national or local ranks, you will choose these two methods:



The Dangerous Way: If you have no time as a business owner to work on the ranking of your website and you wish to purchase SEO services, it is certainly the dangerous way to go. Now even though it’s the dangerous way to go, it can in contrast; be extremely rewarding as well - IF - you and this is rule number one, Choose the right SEO firm. Too many companies will charge you literally an arm and a leg for SEO services, and please - watch out for this. That’s why it’s important to establish a partnership with your SEO team so you both make money in the end.



Always Remember: A Good SEO Company Will Invest THEIR Money IN YOU If They Trust Your Brand To Return Profits! SEO SHOULD NOT COST MORE THAN BRAIN SURGERY, IF IT DOES, THEN GET OUT QUICK!


The Safe Method: If you are like me, and you like to take things the safe but effective way; and not so worried about the time it takes to do so, then you will go with the safe method: DIY SEO. DIY SEO is not as easy as it sounds, not like building a shed from a tutorial off YouTube, and after a bit of a learning curve, you will be able to see the results you want. Here are some simple tasks you can do in your spare time to help rank your website:

  • - Audit your website
  • - Search ‘Niche’ Keywords
  • - Match Your Title and Website Title Metadata
  • - Update your Metadata
  • - Index your Domain for Robots by Adding ‘robots.txt’
  • - Identify the DA of your Competitors
  • - Start a Blog
  • - Post on Social Media Accounts
  • - Solicit your Brand at a Festival
  • - Sell Your Products at Concerts
  • - Schedule for Conventions in your Industry
  • - Reach Out to Competitors for Backlinks (be polite and professional and take ‘no’ well!)


These are just a handful of the numerous ways you can build your business and yes, it does sound daunting but, if you stick to the magic words of the day and to this regard, it’s CONSISTENCY, then you will be sure to see results!



Rebranding may be a strenuous process - but the rewards in the end are worth the efforts. The more your consumers feel a part of the journey of the growth and commitment to your company and what it conveys, the more you will see a growth in your own brand, all because you stuck to the ‘Three C’s!’ Hustle on, my fellow entrepreneurs!
